# 🏓 Patrik Procházka

My name is Patrik Procházka and I come from southern Slovakia. I have always been fascinated by technology. I attended a grammar school, where I gained strong mathematical knowledge and basic programming skills. Later, I continued improving my programming abilities in my free time through self-study and practice. Then I started studying at the Brno University of Technology, which I saw as a challenge and an opportunity for me to expand my knowledge. Currently, I'm in my 3rd year of Bachelor's studies at the Faculty of Information Technology.
